Wind Speed




Wind speed seems to be best understood in conjunction with humidity. The respective plots seem to be the inverse of one another. This could indicate a relationship between how fast air can move and how dense it is.


Furthermore, when we compare wind speed to temperature observations as well as humidity, we are left with greater forces of wind where there appears to be less land area and lower populations. While we do not know if there is a causal relationship between the aforementioned factors, we can recommend further analysis holding them in relationship.
