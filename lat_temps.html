Max Temperature


The data collected reflected our hypothesis. Climate analysis clearly shows that it is indeed warmer near the equator.


With that being said, there is even more the data can tell us. The majority of the plot is north of the equator, but with the most overlapping points occuring at the equator itself. This could be a reflection of greater population densities in these areas, or at the very least a greater concentration of observation points. This makes sense, because the equator is the longest line of latitude there is, with more points of observation and inhabitability than the considerably shorter lines of latitude near the poles. Also, the majority of land area seems to be north of the equator without respect to Antarctica.
