Cloudiness




Cloud cover was spread relatively evenly over the data set. This could be due to the integer scale of the observations, or even their lack of objectivity with respect to how cloudy the skies are and with what category of cloud.


However, even though the data is scattered, there are still trends. Because cloud coverings are grouped tightly across latitudes, we can probably conclude that most fronts move west-east (or vice versa). Perhaps an even more obvious conclusion is that cloud coverage and movement are not related to land area in the same way the observers are restricted to.
